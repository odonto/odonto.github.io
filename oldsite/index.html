OpenOdonto an electronic patient record for Special Care Dentistry

OpenOdonto is an electronic patient record application for Special Care Dentistry, which enables clinicians to access information they need about their patients in one place.

Who We Are

The OpenOdonto CIC is a not-for-profit community interest company, limited by guarantee; set up to ensure that development of OpenOdonto meets the very highest clinical standards.

Guide to OpenOdonto

OpenOdonto is an ambitious, open project, with the aim to design digital tools to address the issues current systems present to dentists providing Special Care Dentistry.

OpenOdonto Development

The OpenOdonto CIC coordinates the development of features that have been requested by users. The costs of development are coordinated by the CIC.
